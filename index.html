  • Forde Campus Tour

School Library

School Library

The Burgmann libraries are a place where students can read books, find information, study, play on computers, draw, and have fun.The Burgmann School library is one library with two branches. Both library spaces are light filled and designed with plenty of flexible learning spaces for large group work through to individual reading nooks. Students are encouraged to borrow books and Oliver, our remote borrowing system, means that reading never has to stop!
